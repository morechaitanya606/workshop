About this experience

Join us for a 2-hour crash course in ceramics. We provide the clay, the tools, and the wine. You provide the creativity. This workshop is designed for complete beginners who want to try their hand at the potter's wheel in a relaxed, social environment.


You'll learn the basics of centering, opening, and pulling walls. By the end of the night, you'll have 1-2 pieces that we will glaze and fire for you to pick up later.
